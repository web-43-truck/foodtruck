Food Truck Owner Persona

Name: John Truck

Profession

  • John is a food truck business owner that is looking for ways to optimize his time and grow his business. He enjoys creating new recipes and serving different areas in the city.

Technology

  • Iphone 15 iOS 17 with Safari as browser
  • MacBook Air with M2 Chip and 8 RAM with Safari as browser
  • Uses Apple Music to stream music from his phone and computer

Attitudes and Behaviors

  • Loves to cook.
  • Loves having a business of his own, a food truck, and the ability to control his schedule.
  • John likes having the ability to bring his business to different locations around the city.
  • He enjoys planning and coming up with ideas to grow his business.
  • He utilizes different social media platforms to provide updates on his food truck location.
  • He hates that he has to constantly update all of his social media applications with his new location.
  • John is optimistic of the future of his business.

Frustrations and Needs

  • John gets frustrated when his socials crash, and he cannot post an updated location.
  • He also gets frustrated when he has to change location mid-work day and has to change location on all socials.
  • John gets upset when he forgets to update his socials on time which leads to losing money on potential customers.
  • He dislikes that he loses time updating applications instead of getting to work
  • He has been searching for a website or application that will make updating his location easier.

Goals

  • He would like to spend less time updating his socials and is looking for a way to update his location in a central location.
  • He would like to spend his time creating new recipes and minimize time on socials
